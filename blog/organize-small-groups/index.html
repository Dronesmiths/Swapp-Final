How to Organize Church Small Groups Effectively

Structure your small group ministry for health and growth. Models, leadership structures, and launch strategies.

Choosing a Model

Before you launch, decided on your model. Are you doing semester-based groups? Free markets groups? Home groups? Sermon-based groups?

  • Consistency is key
  • Align with your church's culture
  • Clear expectations for leaders and members

Recruiting Leaders

Your ministry is only as strong as your leaders. Don't just look for Bible experts; look for facilitators.

  • Look for "FAT" people: Faithful, Available, Teachable
  • Prioritize character over competency
  • Provide clear job descriptions

The Launch Strategy

Launch big. Create momentum seasons (Fall and Spring) where the whole church talks about groups.

  • Host a "GroupLink" event
  • Preach a series on community
  • Sign up in the lobby
