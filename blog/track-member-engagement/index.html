How to Track Member Engagement (Not Just Attendance)

Moving beyond Sunday headcounts. Discover how to measure true engagement through serving, giving, groups, and more.

Defining Engagement

Pastoral care often falls through the cracks, not because of a lack of heart, but a lack of organization. Tracking interactions ensures no one is forgotten during their time of need.

  • Remembering anniversaries of loss
  • Checking in after hospital visits
  • Following up on prayer requests

The Engagement Ladder

Establish a systematic approach to care. Who is responsible for initial contact? Who handles long-term follow-up? Define the workflow.

  • Create care queues for different needs (hospital, grief, financial)
  • Assign ownership to specific pastors or leaders
  • Set automated reminders for follow-up steps

Identifying Disengagement

Pastors can't do it alone. Empower lay leaders to share the burden of care. With the right tools, small group leaders and care teams can be effectively deployed.

  • Share limited access to care notes
  • Train leaders in active listening and prayer
  • Create a feedback loop so pastors stay informed
