How to Increase Church Giving

Practical strategies to cultivate generosity in your church. Move from fundraising to stewardship and see your church resources grow.

Teaching on Generosity

Generosity is a spiritual discipline, not just a financial transaction. When you teach on giving, focus on the heart, not the budget.

  • Connect giving to discipleship
  • Share stories of generosity in the Bible
  • Model generosity as leadership

Transparency Builds Trust

People give to vision, but they trust transparency. Be open about how funds are used.

  • Provide regular financial updates
  • Show exactly where the money goes (e.g., "This offering bought 500 meals")
  • Be accountable and audit your finances

Removing Friction

If it's hard to give, people won't. Remove every barrier you can.

  • Enable mobile giving (text-to-give, app)
  • Allow recurring donations
  • Make sure your giving page loads fast

Celebrating Impact

Always say thank you. Acknowledging gifts prompts recurring generosity.

  • Send automated email receipts
  • Write personal thank-you notes for significant gifts
  • Report back on the impact of their giving
