Where Are Your People?

A list of addresses is just data. A map is insight. When you see where your people live, you understand their context, their commute, and their community.

  • Identify where your core families live
  • See how far people drive to attend
  • Understand the neighborhoods you serve

Demographic Analysis

Strategic planning becomes obvious when you have a map. You can make informed decisions about where to focus your energy and resources.

  • Where should we launch a new campus?
  • Where should we host a summer block party?
  • Where do we need more localized small groups?

Drive-Time Analysis

Community happens locally. Use mapping to create neighborhood-based small groups that actually stick.
